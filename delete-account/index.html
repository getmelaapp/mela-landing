Delete your Mela account

You can delete your Mela account and everything in it at any time, from the app or by email. This page explains both routes, exactly what is removed, and how long any residual copies take to disappear. It applies to the Mela app on iOS and Android.

Delete from the app (fastest)

The quickest way, and it takes effect immediately:

  1. Open Mela and sign in.
  2. Tap your profile picture in the top-right corner.
  3. Open the menu, then Privacy & account.
  4. Tap Delete account, then confirm with your password.

Delete by email (no app needed)

If you cannot access the app, email us from the address on your account with the subject "Delete my account". We verify the request against your account email and delete everything within 30 days, usually much sooner.

support@saymela.com

Delete only some of your data

You do not have to delete your whole account to remove data. Inside the Mela app you can delete individual items at any time, and they are removed from our systems:

  • Delete a contact, along with its card images, from the contact's detail screen.
  • Delete a note or voice memo from the contact it belongs to.
  • Remove your own card images or profile details by editing your profile.

These deletions keep your account active; only the items you choose are removed.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we remove:

  • Your profile: name, company, contact details, and your own business-card images.
  • Every contact you captured, including their card images.
  • All notes, voice memos, and their transcripts.
  • Your events, tags, inventory sheets, and follow-up drafts.
  • Your login credentials and active sessions.

In short: everything tied to your account.

What is kept, and for how long

  • Once you delete, your data is removed from our live systems right away.
  • Residual copies inside encrypted backups age out on a rolling basis within about 30 days, after which they are gone too.
  • Any product feedback you chose to send us is kept with your identity stripped, so it is no longer personal data.

Questions

If a deletion is not working or anything is unclear, email support@saymela.com and we will sort it out. Our Privacy Policy has the full detail on how we handle and retain data.
