Common questions

What is Mela?

Mela is a trade-show lead capture and follow-up app for B2B consumer electronics resellers. You scan business cards, add voice or typed notes, organize contacts by event, and generate personalized follow-up drafts.

The card scan didn't fill in everything correctly. What do I do?

You can always edit any field before saving — scanning is a head start, never a hard requirement. Fields the app wasn't confident about are highlighted so you can double-check them.

How do voice notes work?

Record a short note and Mela transcribes it to text automatically, then suggests relevant hashtags. The text becomes part of the contact's history.

Does Mela send messages for me?

No. Mela generates follow-up drafts and hands them off to your own WhatsApp or email. You review and send every message yourself.

How do I delete a contact or my account?

You can delete any contact from its detail screen in the app. To delete your entire account and all associated data, email us at krantipaudyal@gmail.com and we'll process the request.
