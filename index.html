Built for the booth

A lead memory and follow-up system

Not just a card scanner. Mela captures the context around every lead so your follow-ups actually connect.

📇

Smart Capture

Snap the front and back of a business card. The app reads the details, decodes any QR codes at the same time, and fills an editable contact form within seconds.

🎙️

Voice Notes

Record a quick note between booth visits. Mela transcribes it automatically and pulls out the relevant hashtags for you.

📅

Events

Organize contacts by trade show. Set an active event and every new capture links to it automatically.

🏷️

Tags and Filters

Consumer electronics categories such as brand, product type, grade, and buyer or seller. Filter and search across your whole network.

🕑

Contact History

The same person across multiple shows stays a single contact, with a full relationship timeline instead of duplicates.

✉️

AI Follow-ups

Generate personalized WhatsApp and email drafts that reference shared history and product interests. You review and send every message yourself, and nothing goes out automatically.

How it works

Three steps on the show floor

Under thirty seconds from a handshake to a saved, context-rich lead.

1

Scan the card

Point the camera at the card. Mela reads it and drops the details into a form you can edit.

2

Add the context

Speak a quick note or add tags while the conversation is fresh. It becomes part of the contact.

3

Follow up later

Back at the hotel, generate a personalized draft and send it from your own WhatsApp or email.
