Turn trade-show cards into follow-ups that land.

Mela is a lead capture and follow-up app built for B2B consumer electronics resellers. Scan a card, tag the contact, record a voice note, and let AI draft the follow-up — all at the pace of the show floor.

Built for the booth, not the office

A lead memory and follow-up system — not just a card scanner.

📇

Smart Capture

Snap the front and back of a business card. OCR extracts the details, QR codes are decoded in parallel, and an editable form auto-fills in seconds.

🎙️

Voice Notes

Record a quick note between booth visits. It's transcribed automatically and relevant hashtags are pulled out for you.

📅

Events

Organize contacts by trade show. Set an active event and every new capture links to it automatically.

🏷️

Tags & Filters

Consumer-electronics categories — brand, product type, grade, buyer or seller. Filter and search across your whole network.

🕑

Contact History

The same person across multiple shows stays one contact, with a full relationship timeline instead of duplicates.

✉️

AI Follow-ups

Generate personalized WhatsApp and email drafts that reference shared history and product interests. You send them from your own tools — nothing is sent automatically.
