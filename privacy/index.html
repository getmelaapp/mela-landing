Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Mela ("we", "us") is a trade-show lead capture and follow-up app. This policy explains what information the app collects, how we use it, and who processes it on our behalf. By using Mela, you agree to the practices described here.

Information you provide

  • Account & profile: your name, email address, and optionally your company, job title, phone number, WhatsApp number, website, business address, social links, avatar, and a photo of your own business card.
  • Contacts you capture: business card images (front and back), names, job titles, emails, phone and WhatsApp numbers, company details, social links, and any notes, tags, or hashtags you add.
  • Voice notes: audio you record, which is transcribed to text.
  • Events: trade shows you mark as attending and any events you add.

Device permissions

  • Camera: used only when you photograph a business card or your own card.
  • Microphone: used only when you record a voice note.
  • Photo library: used only if you choose to select an existing card image.

Mela does not access these unless you take an action that requires them.

How we use your information

  • To extract and organize contact details from the cards you scan.
  • To transcribe your voice notes into searchable text.
  • To generate personalized follow-up message drafts for you to review and send.
  • To fetch public company logos so contacts and events are easier to recognize.
  • To operate, secure, and support your account.

Service providers

We use trusted third parties to run the app. They process data only to provide these functions:

  • Supabase — hosts our database, authentication, and file storage (your account data, contacts, card images, and voice recordings).
  • AI processors (OpenAI, Microsoft Azure OpenAI, Anthropic, and/or Google) — process card images and text to extract and normalize contact information, suggest hashtags, and draft follow-up messages.
  • OpenAI Whisper — transcribes your voice notes to text.
  • logo.dev — provides public company logos based on a company's website domain.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising. Mela never sends messages on your behalf — follow-up drafts are handed to your own WhatsApp or email, and you send them yourself. We share data only with the service providers listed above, or if required by law.

Data retention & deletion

Your data is kept while your account is active. You can delete individual contacts at any time from within the app. To delete your entire account and all associated data, email us at krantipaudyal@gmail.com and we will process your request.

Security

Your data is isolated to your own account using row-level security, and access is protected by authentication. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Children's privacy

Mela is a business tool intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email krantipaudyal@gmail.com.
